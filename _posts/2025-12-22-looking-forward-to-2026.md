## Goals for 2026
Before I dive into the goals set out for next year I just want to call out that they have been scaled back over last year. The main reason is because this year I am becoming a father and I want to focus on that experience.

1. Maintain investing savings rate of 28%
  - I lowered this target this year because my wife will be off on maternity leave and I will need to contribute more to the weekly expenses. We do plan on opening an RESP and I will count the contributions towards that.

3. 24 blog posts. Essentially I would like to have 2 posts a month.
  - To me the monthly check-ins are the most important and will likely be half of the posts I make
    - I think I will combine the quarterly check-ins with these as well

4. 12 long form videos on YouTube.
  - I mostly plan on recording the monthlies in video blog form for this purpose. 

5. 50 short videos
  - These will likely be done sporadically with 1 big month of 1 short every day

6. 150 30minute workouts.
  - This is essentially 3 workouts a week. Over 2025 I did terribly in terms of fitness and I want to keep the same goal and hit it this year.

7.  Improve on the blog.

    - Fix the light mode dark mode toggle
