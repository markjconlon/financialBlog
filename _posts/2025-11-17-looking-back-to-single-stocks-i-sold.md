# TD: Toronto-Dominion Bank
Sold a portion in January 2025 and put it all in **BANK**

| Ticker   | Allocation | Gains Since Sale with Dividends |
| -------- | ---------- | ------------------------------- |
| **TD**   | 100%       | 44.39%                          |

Good old TD Bank, now this one I thought about for a very long time and decided to start small with only selling 20% ish of my holdings. Fast forward to July I had watched TD BANK raise up about 25% since I sold in January and I decided to exit the position entirely.

| Ticker   | Allocation | Gains Since Sale with Dividends |

To be honest I was a tad worried about that but at least I am more diversified now. Also 2025 is the first year in a long while that the TSX has outstripped the S&P 500 so if we revert to the mean I'm sure we will be ahead.
